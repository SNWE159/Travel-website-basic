Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and iconic landmarks. Known as "The City of Light, " Paris boasts world-famous attractions such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city is a global center for art, fashion, and gastronomy, with charming neighborhoods like Montmartre and the Marais offering a blend of historic charm and modern chic. Paris' picturesque streets, bustling cafes, and the scenic River Seine create a unique atmosphere that has inspired artists, writers, and travelers for centuries, making it one of the most beloved and visited cities in the world.
